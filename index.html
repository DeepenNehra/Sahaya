Self-Defense

Learn essential self-defense moves to stay safe in any situation.

1. Scream & Create Attention 📢 Why? Attackers fear getting caught.

🛡️ What to do?

*Shout "HELP!" or "BACK OFF!" loudly.

Run toward a crowd or well-lit area.

2. Stomp on the Foot 👣 Why? Hurts the attacker and makes them lose balance.

🛡️ What to do?

Lift your foot and stomp hard on their toes or foot with your heel.

Works best if you're wearing hard shoes.

3. Groin Kick 👊 Why? One of the most painful spots on a man. 🛡️ What to do?

If the attacker is in front, kick them hard between the legs.

Use your knee if they are closer.

This will give you time to run away.

4. Eye Poke or Face Scratch 👀 Why? Causes pain and temporary blindness.

🛡️ What to do?

Use your fingers, nails, or keys to poke or scratch their eyes.

A quick attack can make them let go.

5. Elbow Strike 💪 Why? Strong and easy to use at close range. 🛡️ What to do?

Swing your elbow hard into their face, ribs, or chest.

Works best if someone grabs you from behind or side.

6. Biting (Last Resort) 😬 Why? Unexpected and painful. 🛡️ What to do?

If grabbed, bite their hand, arm, or shoulder as hard as possible.

This will shock them and make them let go.

7. Breaking Free from a Wrist Grab ✋ Why? Many attackers try to grab the wrist first. 🛡️ What to do?

Twist your wrist towards their thumb and pull away quickly. If needed, use your other hand to hit their arm or face.

8. Use Everyday Objects 🔑 Keys: Hold between fingers and punch. 👜 Bag: Swing at the attacker’s face or stomach. 📱 Phone: Use as a hard object to hit their nose or head.

About SAHAYA

🔹 What is SAHAYA?

SAHAYA is a women's safety and tracking system designed to provide security and emergency assistance in critical situations. It uses GPS tracking, emergency alerts, and self-defense resources to help women stay safe in public places.

🔹 Key Features of SAHAYA 1️⃣ Emergency SOS Alert 🚨 Quick Help When Needed

With just one tap, an SOS alert is sent to pre-registered contacts and emergency services. Sends location updates to ensure help arrives quickly.

2️⃣ Real-Time GPS Tracking 📍 Location Safety at All Times

Tracks the user’s real-time location and shares it with trusted contacts.

Helps family members or police locate the user in case of danger.

3️⃣ Safe Route Suggestions 🛣️ Avoid Unsafe Areas

Provides safe route recommendations based on crime reports and street lighting.

Helps users navigate safely at night or in unknown areas.

4️⃣ Self-Defense Training 🥋 Learn to Protect Yourself

Provides videos and step-by-step guides for easy self-defense techniques. Helps women gain confidence to handle dangerous situations.

5️⃣ Emergency Contact Dialing 📞 One-Touch Call for Help

Users can quickly call police (100), women's helpline (1091), or ambulance (108). Helps in situations where typing a message is difficult
